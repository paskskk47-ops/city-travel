Booking & Reservation

How do I book a tour package with Choose Travel?
Booking is simple! You can: (1) Fill our online enquiry form on any package page, (2) Call us at +91 98765 43210, (3) WhatsApp us at +91 98765 43210, or (4) Email us at info@choose.travel. Our team will contact you within 2 hours with full details and pricing.
How far in advance should I book my tour?
We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance for domestic tours, especially during peak seasons (October–March, major Indian holidays). For popular destinations like Kerala, Andaman and Kashmir, booking 4–6 weeks ahead ensures the best hotel availability and pricing.
Can I book a tour for a group?
Absolutely! We specialize in group travel. We cater to corporate groups, family groups, school trips, and social groups of any size. Groups of 10+ travelers get exclusive discounts. Contact us for a customized group tour quote.
Do I need to pay the full amount at the time of booking?
No, you only need to pay a 25% advance at the time of booking to confirm your reservation. The remaining 75% is due 7 days before your trip departure date. For bookings within 7 days of travel, full payment is required upfront.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major payment methods including UPI (PhonePe, GPay, Paytm), Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), Net Banking, and Bank Transfer. For groups, we also accept cheque payments. Our payment gateway is SSL-secured.
Are there any hidden charges?
Absolutely not! We believe in complete transparency. Our quoted price includes everything mentioned in the inclusions. All taxes are included in our package price. We clearly list what's excluded (like flights, travel insurance, personal expenses) so there are no surprises.
Do you offer EMI options?
Yes! We offer EMI on credit card payments for bookings above ₹20,000. EMI options include 3, 6, 9, and 12 month tenures. Standard credit card EMI charges (0–15% interest based on your bank) may apply. Contact us for details.
Do you have a best price guarantee?
Yes! If you find a lower price for the same package (same hotels, same inclusions) elsewhere within 48 hours of booking, we'll match the price or refund the difference. Just share the competing quote and we'll review it immediately.

Cancellation & Refund

What is your cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy:

• 30+ days before trip: Full refund (100%)
• 15–29 days before trip: 75% refund (25% cancellation fee)
• 7–14 days before trip: 50% refund
• Less than 7 days: No refund
• Natural disaster / government ban: Full refund or free rescheduling
How long does the refund take?
Refunds are processed within 5–7 working days from the date of cancellation approval. The amount will be credited back to the original payment source (bank account, card, UPI). You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund is initiated.
Can I reschedule my tour to a different date?
Yes! Rescheduling is allowed with a minimum notice of 15 days before the original trip date. One free rescheduling is permitted. Subsequent changes may attract a rescheduling fee of ₹500–₹2,000 depending on the package. Hotel availability subject to confirmation.

Customization

Can I customize my tour package?
Yes! All our packages are 100% customizable. You can change hotels, add/remove destinations, modify the itinerary, change travel dates, add activities, upgrade to luxury options, or reduce the duration. Use our Plan Your Trip tool or contact us directly.
Do you create tailor-made itineraries for special occasions?
Absolutely! We specialize in honeymoon packages, anniversary trips, birthday surprises, family reunions, and corporate retreats. Just tell us the occasion, your preferences, and budget — and our travel experts will design a perfect experience for you.
Can I add flights to my tour package?
Yes! While most packages are quoted without flights to keep prices flexible, we can add flight bookings to any package. We have access to all major airlines and can often get competitive fares. Ask us for a combined package + flight quote.

During the Trip

Do you provide a tour manager or guide?
Most of our packages include a dedicated driver-guide who is knowledgeable about the destination. Premium packages include a certified local tour guide. For group tours, a dedicated tour manager accompanies the group throughout the trip.
What if there's an emergency during the trip?
We provide 24/7 emergency support during your trip. Your trip includes a dedicated support number that you can call at any time. We're connected to local hospitals, police, and emergency services in all our destinations. We also recommend purchasing travel insurance (available as add-on).
Is travel insurance included in the packages?
Travel insurance is available as an optional add-on for ₹299–₹599 per person. It covers medical emergencies, trip cancellation, baggage loss, and delay. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance, especially for adventure or long-duration packages.
