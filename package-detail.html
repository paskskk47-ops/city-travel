OVERVIEW

Experience the magical backwaters of Kerala on a traditional houseboat. Sail through serene canals lined with palm trees, witness rustic village life, and savour authentic Kerala cuisine prepared fresh on board. This 4-day itinerary combines the tranquil backwaters of Alleppey with the misty tea gardens of Munnar and the historic sights of Cochin for a truly unforgettable holiday.

ITINERARY

Day 1 Arrival in Cochin - Transfer to Munnar

Arrive at Cochin Airport/Railway Station where our driver will greet you. Embark on a picturesque 4-hour drive to Munnar, enjoying waterfalls, spice gardens, and mist-clad hills en route. Check-in to your resort and relax for the evening. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 2 Munnar Sightseeing

After a wholesome breakfast, explore Eravikulam National Park (home to Nilgiri Tahr), Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, and the Tea Museum. Stroll around local spice markets in the evening. Overnight stay in Munnar.

Day 3 Munnar to Alleppey Houseboat Cruise

Drive down to Alleppey (approx. 4.5 hours) and board your private luxury houseboat by 12:00 noon. Cruise past scenic paddy fields, village homes, and coconut lagoons. Relish traditional Kerala lunch, evening tea with snacks, and dinner freshly prepared on board. Overnight stay on houseboat.

Day 4 Departure from Cochin

Enjoy morning breakfast on the tranquil waters before checking out at 9:00 AM. Drive to Cochin for quick sightseeing (Fort Kochi, Marine Drive) and drop-off at Cochin Airport/Railway Station for your onward journey with wonderful memories!
