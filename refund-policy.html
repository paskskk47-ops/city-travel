Refund & Cancellation Policy

Home›Refund Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026 | Last Updated: July 1, 2026

At Choose Travel, we understand that plans can change unexpectedly due to personal emergencies, health issues, or work commitments. Our Refund & Cancellation Policy is designed to be as transparent, fair, and flexible as possible while respecting our financial commitments to hotels, houseboats, and transport providers.

Customer-First Approach: We offer 100% free rescheduling or full refunds in the event of government-imposed travel bans or severe natural disasters. Your peace of mind is our highest priority.

1. Standard Cancellation Fee Matrix

If you decide to cancel your confirmed tour package, the cancellation charges will be calculated based on the number of days remaining between the date of written cancellation notice and your scheduled trip departure date:

Time of Cancellation Cancellation Fee Refund Amount
30+ Days before departure ₹1,500 / person (Admin fee) 100% Refund (minus admin fee)
15 to 29 Days before departure 25% of total package cost 75% Refund
7 to 14 Days before departure 50% of total package cost 50% Refund
Less than 7 Days / No-Show 100% of total package cost No Refund

2. Special & Peak Season Cancellation Rules

Certain travel periods and specialized accommodations operate under strict non-refundable terms imposed by hotel resorts and houseboat unions:

  • Peak Holiday Dates (Dec 20 – Jan 5 & Diwali): Bookings traveling during major festive periods are subject to 100% cancellation charges if cancelled within 30 days of departure, as luxury resorts and houseboats do not offer refunds during peak demand dates.
  • Alleppey Premium Houseboats: Private houseboat bookings confirmed for weekends or peak dates may incur a minimum 50% cancellation fee regardless of the cancellation window, in accordance with local houseboat association rules.
  • Flight & Train Tickets: If your package includes domestic flight or railway tickets booked through us, cancellation fees for those tickets will be governed strictly by the respective airline's or Indian Railways' (IRCTC) refund rules, in addition to our package cancellation terms.

3. Rescheduling & Trip Postponement

We want to help you take your dream trip even if your dates need to shift:

  • One Free Rescheduling: You may postpone your trip to a future date (within 6 months of original travel date) with zero rescheduling fees, provided written notice is received at least 15 days prior to departure.
  • Rate Differences: If your rescheduled trip falls into a higher tariff season (e.g., shifting from monsoon season to winter peak season), the difference in hotel and houseboat tariffs will be charged extra. If it shifts to a lower tariff season, the price difference will be adjusted against your balance or refunded.

4. Force Majeure & Natural Disasters

In the rare event that your trip cannot commence due to uncontrollable circumstances — including severe natural disasters (floods, cyclones, earthquakes), government-imposed travel lockdowns, or official tourism department advisories closing a destination — Choose Travel will offer:

  • 100% Free Date Change: Reschedule your trip to any date within 12 months without any administrative or cancellation charges.
  • Full Credit Voucher: Receive a travel credit voucher worth 100% of your paid amount, transferable to immediate family members and valid for any Choose Travel package across India.
  • Cash Refund: Where hotels and ground suppliers agree to refund advance deposits without penalty, we will issue a full monetary refund within 14 working days.

5. How to Initiate a Cancellation or Refund

To request a cancellation or trip rescheduling, follow these simple steps:

  1. Send a formal written cancellation request via email to support@choose.travel from your registered booking email address. Include your Booking Reference ID, Traveler Name, and reason for cancellation.
  2. Our operations team will acknowledge your request within 24 working hours and calculate the applicable refund amount based on the cancellation timestamp.
  3. Once approved, the refund amount will be credited back to your original payment method (Bank Account NEFT/RTGS, Credit Card, or UPI) within 5 to 7 working days.
